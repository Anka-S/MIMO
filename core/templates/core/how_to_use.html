How to Use PlanApp

  • Create a Task: Click on "Create New Task" to add a task. Fill in the title, set the priority, and choose a due date.
  • Edit a Task: Click the "Edit" button next to any task to update its details.
  • Mark a Task as Complete: Once a task is done, click "Mark as Complete" to move it to the completed section.
  • Delete a Task: Click "Delete" to remove a task from your list permanently.
  • Search Tasks: Use the search bar to quickly find tasks by title.
